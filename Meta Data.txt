=========================================
POWER BI PROJECT METADATA
=========================================

1. DIMENSION TABLES (Lookup & Filtering)
-----------------------------------------
Table: dim_date
- Fields: date, fiscal_year, fy_month_num, month, quarters, ytd...
- Purpose: Time intelligence and chronological filtering.

Table: dim_customer
- Fields: channel, customer, customer_code, market, platform

- Fields: market, region, sub_zone
- Purpose: Geographical and market segmentation.
